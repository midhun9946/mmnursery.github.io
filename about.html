MM Nursery as a invention.

We started our Journey since long back might not be in this generation, but the momentum continues years and years. It’s our strong belief that we are the one who introduced Mangosteen plant in Kerala long back.

Our great Grandfather late Mr.jacob Moothedan as a traveler and Entrepreneur collected the seeds of Mangosteen and Ramboottan from south east countries in late 19th centuries and believes started producing the plant without any profit motive.

Time passed away and it’s our turn to make holistic approach towards our profession as plant producers and believes we are on the right way to comply the professional mannerism as the time demands and do keep in mind the way we evolved.
